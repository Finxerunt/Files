Aishah Daiyan

Scholar at the International Socioeconomics Laboratory

Aishah Daiyan is a current member of FINXERUNT’s digital marketing committee and joined the organization in the Spring of 2021. She is a goal-driven person who spends most of her free time striving to help make her community better. To her, community is an important part of her life because it not only connects her to the people around her, but also to new ideas that can impact her life. She first gained interest in socioeconomics when she noticed the large gaps in income and lack of diversity that communities around her had. She believes that socio-economics has a huge influence over our lives and it is vital that we pay close attention to issues related to it. Aishah claims these issues related to economic inequality and social segregation are often ignored in disadvantaged communities to encourage development in other succeeding ones. Progressing towards socio-economic issues such as enforcing certain regulations and making sure that everyone has uniform access to the same things are consequential to achieve equality. Aishah joined FINXERUNT to help combat these issues through their influential platform and believes that through her work here, she is one step closer to implementing change.
